Security disclosures

If you have identified a potential security vulnerability in CAPA Resolve's infrastructure or website, please report it responsibly. Do not publish details publicly before reaching out. Contact us at legal@caparesolve.com with "Security Disclosure" in the subject line, and we will respond promptly. See also the Security page for more information on our security approach.

Response expectations

We are a small team in active platform preparation. We aim to respond to all enquiries within two business days. Legal and compliance matters may take longer depending on complexity.

We do not provide legal advice to third parties via email or any other channel. If your enquiry relates to your own dispute or legal situation, please consult a qualified legal professional.
