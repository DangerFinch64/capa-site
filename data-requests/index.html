Data Requests

How to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information held by CAPA Resolve.

Last updated: April 2026

Your rights

Depending on where you are located and the applicable law, you may have rights regarding personal information that CAPA Resolve holds about you. These may include:

  • Access: the right to request a copy of personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Deletion: the right to request deletion of personal information in certain circumstances
  • Restriction: the right to request that we restrict processing of your personal information in certain circumstances
  • Portability: the right to receive your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format in certain circumstances
  • Objection: the right to object to certain types of processing of your personal information

These rights are subject to applicable law and may be limited in certain circumstances. We will respond to all valid requests within the timeframe required by applicable law (typically one calendar month for requests under UK/EU GDPR).

How to submit a request

Please direct all data subject requests to:

privacy@caparesolve.com

When submitting a request, please include:

  • Your name and contact details
  • The type of request (access, correction, deletion, etc.)
  • Enough detail for us to identify the information your request relates to

We may need to verify your identity before processing a request. We will ask only for what is reasonably necessary to do so.

Law enforcement and third-party requests

CAPA Resolve will only disclose personal information to law enforcement or other third parties in response to a valid legal obligation (such as a court order or statutory notice). We do not voluntarily disclose user information to law enforcement without legal compulsion, and we will seek to notify users of requests where we are legally permitted to do so.

Third parties (other than law enforcement) may not request personal information about other individuals through this process. Such requests will not be fulfilled.

Complaints

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your personal information or a data request, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority. In the UK, this is the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO): ico.org.uk. We would, however, ask that you contact us first so we have an opportunity to address your concern.

Contact

For all data-related enquiries: privacy@caparesolve.com
For other contact addresses, see the Contact page.
