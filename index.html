What we do

Helping people take control of their disputes

Whether you are dealing with a service failure, a billing dispute, an unresolved complaint, or a situation where an organisation has not met its obligations, CAPA provides structured tools and guidance to help you build a clear, credible case.

  • Organise your case Bring together the facts, evidence, correspondence, and timeline that matter. A well-organised case is the foundation of any effective resolution.
  • Identify your options Understand what policies, regulations, and escalation pathways apply to your situation. Know where you stand before you act.
  • Take user-directed action CAPA supports your decisions — it does not make them for you. You stay in control of every step and every disclosure.
  • Maintain a clear audit trail Keep a structured record of what happened, when, and what was communicated. Documentation is often the difference between a resolved and an unresolved case.
