Current status

The CAPA Resolve public website is operational. The full platform is currently in active preparation and has not yet been released to general availability. At this stage, there are no production platform services to report status on.

Platform rollout

CAPA Resolve is being built and will be made available progressively. When platform services are live, we intend to provide a dedicated, real-time status page that reflects the operational status of each service component. This page will be updated or redirected when that experience is available.

Reporting an issue

If you are experiencing a problem with this website or have an operational concern, please contact us directly:

  • Email: support@caparesolve.com
  • For security-related concerns, see the Security page

We do not have an automated incident reporting or alerting system in place at this stage. All operational enquiries are handled by the team directly.
